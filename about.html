About Turnstone Ltd

Healthcare Data Intelligence

Solving the Healthcare Data Translation Problem

Your clinical team needs standardized research data. Your IT department is managing legacy systems. Your business stakeholders want measurable ROI. Everyone speaks a different language, and critical initiatives stall in the translation.

I specialize in bridging that gap. With 15+ years working across federal agencies, academic medical centers, and life sciences companies, I help healthcare organizations transform fragmented data systems into platforms that support clinical research, real-world evidence, and observational studies.

What I Do

I don't just implement technology—I facilitate the conversations that make projects succeed. When your OMOP implementation stalls because clinical and IT teams can't agree on priorities, I help find the path forward. When your cloud migration feels overwhelming, I break it into manageable phases. When stakeholders can't see the forest for the trees, I restore project context and drive issues to resolution.

Areas of Focus:
  • OMOP Common Data Model Implementation - Assessment, roadmap development, technical execution, and validation
  • Cloud Healthcare Architecture - Azure, AWS, and Oracle Cloud migration strategy and implementation
  • Clinical Research Data Platforms - Real-world evidence infrastructure and observational study design
  • Legacy System Modernization - Practical approaches to moving aging applications to modern platforms
  • Stakeholder Alignment - Facilitating productive conversations between clinical, IT, and business teams
